MBM - Mini Business Manager is the simplest way to track your service business earnings and know your true hourly rate.

Perfect for landscapers, cleaners, handymen, dog walkers, tutors, and anyone running a small service business.

TRACK YOUR REAL HOURLY RATE
Enter your daily revenue, expenses, and hours worked. MBM instantly calculates your net profit and actual hourly rate - so you always know what you're really earning.

SCHEDULE & MANAGE JOBS
Keep track of upcoming jobs with a clean calendar view. See which days were profitable (green) or not (red) at a glance. Tap any day to see scheduled jobs and mark them complete.

MANAGE CUSTOMERS
Store customer details, service prices, and contact info. One tap to call, text, or get directions to their address.

AUTOMATIC CLOUD SYNC
Your data automatically syncs across all your Apple devices via iCloud. No account needed - it just works.

- Daily revenue, expense, and hours tracking
- Real-time hourly rate calculation
- Calendar with profit/loss visualization
- Customer management with contact integration
- Job scheduling and completion tracking
- Daily reminder notifications
- iCloud sync across devices
- Clean, simple interface

Built for busy service professionals who want to understand their business without complicated software.
