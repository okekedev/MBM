Privacy Policy

MBM - Mini Business Manager

Overview

MBM ("Mini Business Manager") is designed with your privacy in mind. We believe your business data belongs to you and only you.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data. MBM does not have servers, accounts, or any mechanism to access your information.

Data Storage

All data you enter in MBM is stored:

  • Locally on your device using Apple's secure storage
  • In your personal iCloud account (if you have iCloud enabled) for syncing across your devices

Your data never passes through our servers because we don't have any servers. The iCloud sync is handled entirely by Apple using your personal Apple ID.

Data We Don't Collect

  • Personal information (name, email, phone)
  • Business or financial data
  • Customer information
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics
  • Device identifiers

Third-Party Services

MBM uses Apple's iCloud service for optional data syncing. This is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Data Deletion

To delete all your data, simply delete the MBM app from your device. If you use iCloud sync, you can also remove MBM data from your iCloud account in Settings > Apple ID > iCloud > Manage Storage.

Children's Privacy

MBM does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed for business professionals.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will post the new version here with an updated date.
