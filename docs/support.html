Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my daily earnings?

On the Today tab, enter your revenue, expenses, and hours worked. MBM automatically calculates your net profit and hourly rate.

Does my data sync across devices?

Yes! If you're signed into iCloud on your devices, MBM automatically syncs your data. No account or setup needed.

How do I add a new customer?

Go to the Customers tab and tap the + button. Enter their name, service details, and contact info.

How do I schedule a job?

Go to the Calendar tab, tap on a day, then tap the + button to add a job for that date.

What do the colors on the calendar mean?

Green days had positive net profit. Red days had negative net profit (expenses exceeded revenue).

How do I set up daily reminders?

Go to Settings and enable Daily Reminder. Choose what time you want to be reminded to log your day.

How do I delete my data?

To delete all data, simply delete the app. To remove iCloud data, go to iPhone Settings > Apple ID > iCloud > Manage Storage > MBM.

Is my data private?

Yes. All data stays on your device and your personal iCloud account. We don't have servers and cannot access your information.
